Improved transfer learning using textural features conflation and dynamically fine-tuned layers

This study enhances transfer learning by selecting data points with minimal textural feature divergence and optimally fine-tuning pre-trained model layers, using measures of divergence. Tested on nine datasets with five models, this approach improves accuracy by focusing on data points and layers with characteristics closely matching the original tasks, achieving notable accuracy gains. Combining both methods further increases effectiveness, demonstrating more precise adaptation and reduced trial-and-error in layer selection.
